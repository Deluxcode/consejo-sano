Careers

ConsejoSano is the leading Hispanic health platform in the US, helping clients to increase engagement, simplify care, and lower costs related to their Spanish­-speaking members. We’re on a mission to remove the #1 cause of healthcare disparities language and culture barriers and to help people live better, healthier lives.

Our current services include telehealth, care navigation, and outbound engagement and education campaigns, all of which are delivered by native­ Spanish­-speaking, Hispanic doctors. Our service is totally unique in the US healthcare market, which gives us the opportunity to build an extremely valuable business while providing a life­ changing resource to underserved communities.

We have a client list that includes some of the leading employers, health plans, and IPAs in the country, and a technical platform that is secure, flexible, and built to scale quickly. With over 30,000 members and partnerships with Mercer and other key channel partners, we have significant traction and are poised for a period of rapid growth across all verticals.

Item 1

We are looking for an experienced Product Manager with proven success to provide creative strategy and leadership to the most promising venture-backed, healthcare technology startup in Southern California. The Product Manager will oversee full product development, improvement of customer experience, and market positioning strategy through market research; big data analysis; team collaboration; and contribution of overall product expertise. We have an ambitious product vision and are looking for the right expert to serve as the team lead in empowering and building a successful roadmap for its conceptualization, design, and development.

The right candidate will have an interest in taking the next step in their own career in product management to facilitate development of the ConsejoSano product offering. A detail-oriented, positive attitude, team player with a strong passion for developing and implementing product strategies will be a strong fit for the Product Manager role.

We are developing a product that has changed, and will continue to improve, the lives of our users. We have assembled a team of passionate and intelligent individuals who genuinely enjoy coming to work each day. As we look to the next 18 to 24 months and beyond, we are excited to build out our platform and find an ambitious and futuristic team lead to grow and learn with us along the way.

Essential Duties & Core Responsibilities:

  • Support the strategy and vision of the entire company in order to drive product definition and continue to position ConsejoSano as an instrumental in the industry
  • Serve as a creative leader in facilitating product change to meet and exceed current standards through QA analysis and lead team discussion
  • Analyze market data and competition in order to deliver unique product value
  • Translate customer product requests and needs to engineering and sales staff as well as develop an implementation timeline to improve the customer
  • Serve as an active member of our operations team and be viewed as a problem solver
  • Serve as a liaison among senior leadership and management in supporting and guiding the product vision and building trust among stakeholders
  • Collaborate with a diverse team and ensure situational awareness to promote knowledge, understanding, and product goal development
  • Lead a cross-functional team and ensure that the product is designed for specific needs, but with wider applications in mind
  • Develop and promote marketing strategies that will support the company mission
  • Provide clear, effective communication of objectives and issues as they arise
  • Be committed to the professional development of your direct product development reports and the overall learning of non-technical team members development; be seen as an approachable resource who facilitates knowledge sharing and encourages a culture of intellectual curiosity
  • Be a student of current industry trends, ensuring that the company maintains and expands its technology leadership position
  • Additional responsibilities as per the evolving needs of the organization

Basic Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 5+ years of product management or product marketing experience in the healthcare or technology space
  • Experience in client-facing roles and an ability to understand evolving customer needs
  • Strong analytical, project management, and problem solving skills
  • Close attention to detail and experience with analytical tools
  • Startup/small company experience with proven track record of successful product launch and management

Strongly preferred:

  • Technical degree and/or MBA
  • Bilingual in Spanish and/or other languages aside from English
  • Previous Health IT experience including exposure to providers & health plans
  • Prior experience in big-data analysis and understanding of machine-learning tools/natural language processing
  • Understanding of and experience managing or launching HIPAA-compliant products

Successful candidates will have:

  • A desire to lead product ideation, gather and analyze big-data, and strategize market positioning for a digital health start-up
  • A strong strategic and operational skill set
  • Desire to take ownership of the full product life cycle
  • Professional and positive approach, self-motivated, strong in building relationships, great at being a team player, dynamic and engaging demeanor, creative and innovative
  • Exceptional problem solving and communication skills

Some of the great perks of working at ConsejoSano include:

  • Three weeks of paid vacation per year and eight annual holidays with two additional flex holidays
  • Superb medical/dental/vision benefits with significant contributions toward covering all dependents
  • Competitive salary and equity
  • Healthy snacks
  • Sponsored team outings (we are in one of the coolest neighborhoods in all the land)
  • The ability to truly have a positive impact on people who need it most
  • The support of a highly dedicated team focused on building the future of health care

About the Company:

At ConsejoSano, we like to think of ourselves as constantly pushing the envelope to solve any number of issues for our users as they work to improve their own health. Our platform is used by our enterprise clients to enable patients to get the right care, at the right time, in the right place, and in the right way. We take pride in improving health outcomes through interacting with patients in revolutionary ways that are convenient to them and fit into their daily lives. With social determinants of health rapidly becoming an area of focus for our healthcare system, ConsejoSano is perfectly positioned to address these issues and use technology to scale our ability to help people live healthier lives.

ConsejoSano was started approximately three years ago and is headquartered in the NoHo Arts district of Los Angeles County. Our team includes nationally recognized serial entrepreneurs, physicians, nurses, and an incredible team of sales and operations professionals. We raised our Series A in January of this year and are supported by top tier investors and some of the most recognized names in health care.
